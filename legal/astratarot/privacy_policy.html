Privacy Policy

Astra Tarot
Developer: Nikomiko
Effective Date: July 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Astra Tarot. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application ("App"). Please read this policy carefully to understand our practices.

Information We Do Not Collect

Astra Tarot does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. Specifically:

  • We do not require user accounts or registration.
  • We do not collect names, email addresses, or contact information.
  • We do not have server-side storage or cloud databases.
  • We do not track your location.
  • We do not access your contacts, photos, or other device data.

Local Data Storage

The App stores certain data locally on your device using SharedPreferences. This data never leaves your device and includes:

  • App settings — your preferred language and display preferences.
  • Streak data — your daily reading streak count and dates.
  • Crystal balance — your virtual currency balance.
  • Reading history — your past tarot reading results.

This data is stored exclusively on your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services that may collect certain data:

Google AdMob (Advertising)
We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect:

  • Device identifiers (advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • General device information (model, OS version)
  • Ad interaction data

This data is used for ad personalization and analytics. For more information, please review Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Google Play Billing / Apple In-App Purchases
In-app purchases are processed through the respective platform's billing system (Google Play Store or Apple App Store). We do not have access to your payment information. Please refer to the platform's privacy policy for details:

  • Google Play: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Apple: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties. The only data that may be collected is through AdMob as described above, which is governed by Google's own privacy policies.

Children's Privacy

Astra Tarot is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe that a child under 13 has provided us with personal data, please contact us at the email below, and we will take steps to remove such information.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the App with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
